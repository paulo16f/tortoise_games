GAME DOCUMENTATION

Everything in Depthbreaker — systems, numbers, and rules. Use the sidebar to jump.

Introduction

Depthbreaker is a browser-based multiplayer dungeon RPG. You share one persistent dungeon with everyone on your server: fight, gather, cook, complete dailies, trade on the player market, and push the Depth counter as far as it goes. Progress (level, bank, gold, skins) persists across runs and devices.

Account & Identity

  • Guest — instant play; progress persists in your browser session.
  • Email — upgrade in place anytime; your guest progress carries over.
  • Wallet linking (SIWS) — link a Solana wallet by signing a message. Signing is free, moves no funds, and is never required to log in. It will gate the token-side market when the token launches.
The game only ever asks your wallet to SIGN a message. It never asks for your seed phrase and never initiates transfers by itself.

The Dungeon & Depth

  • Each server room is a seeded dungeon: combat rooms, resource rooms, a market room, and a boss.
  • Killing the floor boss raises Depth for the whole room and announces it in world chat.
  • Per depth: enemies gain ~20% health and ~15% damage, and pay ~25% more XP and ~35% more gold.
  • The minimap (top-right) shows layout, enemies, party, resources, and the market stall.

Official Map (coming)

The world is expanding into a spawn town surrounded by three leveled areas (Lv 1-10, 10-20, 30-40), each with its own minions, elites, and area boss — plus the Coliseum: a world boss that grows permanently stronger every time it dies, until no lone breaker can face it.

Combat Rules

  • Auto-attack on a swing timer; skills respect a ~1s global cooldown (defensives are off-GCD panic buttons).
  • Skill presses just before a cooldown ends are buffered — mash friendly.
  • Elite/boss ground slams are telegraphed with a ring; leaving the ring avoids all damage.
  • Threat is real: healing draws aggro, taunts force targets, running away leashes enemies.

Dailies & Streaks

  • 3 quests per day, same set for everyone, rotating deterministically by date.
  • Rewards: 40–70 gold + XP each. Claim in-game (J).
  • Consecutive-day claims build a streak; the streak multiplies daily gold. Missing a day resets it.

Economy Overview

Gold in: kills (depth-scaled, capped per run), dailies + streaks, selling loot/resources, wheel prizes.
Gold out: gear and potions at the NPC market, cosmetic skins (200–600g), player trades.
All earnings are validated server-side and capped per account per day — no client can print gold.

Trading Post

  • Open anywhere with T. Items list OUT of your bank (escrowed) at your price.
  • Buying settles atomically: gold moves buyer → seller, item moves to the buyer's bank.
  • Sales pay a small market fee — the economy's sink.

Gold Exchange

The 💰 Gold tab lets you list in-game gold at a USD price. The gold escrows out of your wallet while listed; cancel any time to get it back. Buying a listing settles on-chain in the seller's favor (95% seller / 5% treasury) — this unlocks together with wallet linking when the token launches.

The Token

  • The game never mints or pays out tokens. The only way tokens move is player-to-player: buying gold listings, and optional premium spends.
  • Premium spends (like paid wheel spins) split 50% burned / 50% treasury.
  • Treasury funds events, development, and operations.
  • Contract address will be published here at launch. Anything else claiming to be the token is fake.

Fortune Wheel

  • One free spin per 24h (G). 20 segments: gold jackpot (120g, ~1 in 20), crystal shards, potions, food, ore.
  • If your stash is full, item prizes auto-convert to their gold value — a spin never pays nothing.
  • Paid spins arrive with the token (priced in USD, 50% burn / 50% treasury).

Bank & Storage

  • Bank at the market stall (N): 24 slots, stacks to 999, persists forever.
  • Your run bag is temporary — bank what matters before you log off.
  • Trading Post listings escrow from the bank, so banked goods are your tradable wealth.

World Chat

C focuses chat; everyone in your room sees it. Depth breakthroughs and boss events are announced here. Rate-limited against spam.

FAQ

Is this pay-to-win? No. Everything that affects power is earned in-game with gold. The token side is trading and cosmetics-adjacent spending, always player-to-player.

Do I need a wallet to play? No. Guest and email play are full-featured. A wallet only gates the token-side market once it launches.

I died — did I lose my stuff? Your bank is always safe. You respawn in a few seconds on the same floor.

The market won't let me sell. Stand next to the market stall for NPC sales; Trading Post (T) works anywhere but lists from your bank.
