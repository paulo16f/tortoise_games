HOW TO PLAY DEPTHBREAKER

From your first login to breaking depth and trading — written for players, not developers.

Start Here: Your First Run

Play as a guest instantly, or register with email so your progress is safe anywhere. Pick a class, choose your champion's form (each class has two), name them, and Enter World.

  • WASD or click the ground to move. Click a monster to target it; press 1 to toggle auto-attack.
  • Skills sit on keys 1–0. They unlock automatically as you level — check the Skill Book (K).
  • The market stall in the starting room buys your loot and sells potions and gear. Cooking station and bank are right there too.
Tip: your health orb is bottom-left, XP orb bottom-right. If it turns red, run — dying is cheap but time is gold.

The Depth

The dungeon has a floor boss. Kill it and the floor gives way — Depth +1. Every depth makes enemies stronger and richer: more XP, more gold, better odds. How deep you go is the whole game.

Deeper enemies hit harder. Bring a group past Depth 3 — or be very good.

Combat

  • Auto-attack runs on a swing timer; skills weave between swings and share a global cooldown.
  • Elites and bosses telegraph a ground slam — step out of the ring or eat heavy damage.
  • Watch the boss portal timer in the top-left chip. When the boss walks out, the whole floor knows.

Gathering & Cooking

  • Iron veins and crystal veins — click to mine (ore sells; crystal sells better).
  • Fishing spots — regular and deep. Cook raw fish at the stall's cooking station (F): cooked fish heals and sells for more.
  • Getting hit interrupts gathering. Clear the room first.

Dailies & Streaks

Three daily quests rotate every day (mine X, slay X, reach depth X, cook X…). Claim in the quest panel (J). Claiming on consecutive days builds a streak that boosts the gold reward — don't break the chain.

Economy & Trading

Gold is earned by playing — kills (scaled by depth), dailies, selling loot and resources. Gold is spent on gear, potions, and cosmetic skins.

  • Trading Post (T) — the player market. List items from your bank at your price; other players buy them with gold.
  • 💰 Gold tab — list your gold for other players to buy. Purchases unlock with wallet linking (coming with the token).
  • Bank (N) — persistent storage at the stall; survives every run. Tradable goods live here.
  • Daily gold earnings are capped per account — the economy stays fair for everyone.

Fortune Wheel

One free spin every 24 hours (G). Twenty segments — most pay resources, one pays a fat stack of gold. It never costs anything to spin your free spin.
